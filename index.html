Rapid DevOps LLC
App Name: My AI Crush
Effective Date: 01 June 2025

1. Introduction

Rapid DevOps LLC respects your right to privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide through our application. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use, store, and protect it, as well as your rights regarding this data.

2. Data Collection and Use

We collect the following categories of data:

  • Profile Information: name, gender, age, and interests.
  • User Identifier associated with your Apple account via OAuth.
  • Chat History with the AI bot: text messages exchanged within the app.

These data are used for:

  • Personalizing characters and dialogues;
  • Enhancing interaction with AI;
  • Providing app functionality;
  • Processing purchases (if applicable).

3. Data Storage

All data is stored on a server located in the Netherlands. We implement technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data retention period:

  • As long as you continue using the app;
  • Up to 30 days after account deletion (upon request).

4. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except when necessary to provide services:

  • OpenAI API: chat messages may be sent to OpenAI to generate AI responses. OpenAI processes this data according to its own privacy policy. We do not send your name, age, or other profile data directly to OpenAI.

5. Payments and Subscriptions

If your app includes in-app purchases or subscriptions, these are processed via Apple App Store or Apple ID. We do not store payment information on our servers.

6. Children and Age Restrictions

Our app is intended for users over the age of 13. The recommended age for use is 17+, due to the nature of content (emotional and romantic interaction). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your data;
  • Request correction or deletion of your data;
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (account deletion);
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority.
